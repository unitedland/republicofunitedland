Become a Citizen of the Republic

Citizenship in the Republic of United Land represents a commitment to our shared values of democratic governance, mutual respect, and collective progress. The Republic welcomes all individuals who demonstrate alignment with our founding principles, regardless of origin.

Pathways to Citizenship

Path A Naturalization

Available to any individual worldwide. Submit a formal application, complete the Civic Knowledge Assessment, take the Oath of Allegiance, and receive approval from the Citizenship Review Board.

Path B Birthright

Children born to at least one citizen of the Republic are entitled to citizenship by birth, per Article VI of the Constitution.

Path C Special Grant

The National Assembly may, by majority vote, confer honorary citizenship upon individuals who have made extraordinary contributions to the Republic or its mission.

Requirements for Naturalization

  • Application — Complete the official citizenship application form through the Ministry of Interior
  • Civic Assessment — Demonstrate knowledge of the Constitution, governance structure, and national values
  • Character Attestation — Provide references or attestation of good character and civic commitment
  • Oath of Allegiance — Take the formal oath to uphold and defend the Constitution of the Republic
  • Review Board Approval — Final approval by the Citizenship Review Board of the Ministry of Interior

Application Process

Submit Application

Complete the online application through the Government Portal with required personal information and statement of intent.

Document Review

The Ministry of Interior reviews your application and supporting materials within 14 business days.

Civic Assessment

Complete the Civic Knowledge Assessment covering the Constitution, government structure, and national values.

Oath Ceremony

Participate in the Oath of Allegiance ceremony, administered by the Ministry of Interior.

Certificate of Citizenship

Receive your official Certificate of Citizenship and be enrolled in the National Registry.

Rights of Citizens

🗳️
Right to vote in all national elections and referenda
📜
Full protection under the Constitution and Bill of Rights
🏛️
Right to hold public office and serve in government
🛡️
Access to consular assistance and diplomatic protection
📣
Freedom of expression, assembly, and petition
⚖️
Right to due process and equal protection under law

Processing Time

Applications are typically reviewed within 14 business days. Expedited processing may be available for qualifying applicants.

No Fee Required

Citizenship in the Republic of United Land carries no application fee. The Republic believes citizenship is a right, not a commodity.
